I am a researcher specializing in soft computing, evolutionary algorithms, quantum computing, and mathematical optimization, with a keen interest in both their theoretical foundations and practical applications. I have recently submitted my Ph.D. thesis, supervised by Prof. C. Vasantha Lakshmi and Prof. C. Patvardhan at Dayalbagh Educational Institute in Agra, India. My doctoral research focused on the design and analysis of socio-inspired evolutionary algorithms for solving complex optimization problems.

My research combines principles from evolutionary computation, social behavior modeling, and optimization to improve the performance and adaptability of computational algorithms. I have published my findings in peer-reviewed journals and have presented at various national and international conferences. One of my recent publications, titled "Socio-inspired Evolutionary Algorithms: A Unified Framework and Survey," has garnered positive feedback and is seeing an increasing number of citations.

I am actively seeking postdoctoral opportunities that enable me to contribute to and collaborate on research in fields such as artificial intelligence, quantum information theory, soft computing, optimization theory, and cryptography. I have a strong passion for developing solutions that are not only mathematically rigorous but also have a meaningful impact on real-world applications.
